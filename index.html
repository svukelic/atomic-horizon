Exosuit Tactical Skirmish

A lightning-fast co-op skirmish game where you drop into hostile territory in a customizable exosuit, fighting through asymmetric scenarios where survival depends on preparation, positioning, and your squad.

What you do
Each player controls a single exosuit operator. You deploy into missions—blow through infantry positions, breach fortified compounds, take down war machines. When heavy threats arrive, you adapt: use cover, coordinate fire, exploit the terrain. Fast when you're winning, brutal when you're tested.

Between missions
  • Your exosuit persists — swap your weapons, modify your loadout, adapt to a new combat role.
  • Your operator grows — track mission log of your actions: victories, failures, and atrocities, thus establishing your reputation

The game
  • 60-120 minute co-op scenarios for 2-5 players
  • Card-based mechanics — no rulebook lookups mid-firefight
  • Gear defines your deck — your equipment determines your cards and what you bring to the fight
  • Dice-driven combat — tactical positioning meets visceral rolling
  • Asymmetric missions — you're outnumbered, outgunned, but not outmaneuvered
  • Mission-based play — standalone scenarios for quick deployment, or link them into campaigns where your choices persist

Unlike Warhammer, Infinity or Battletech
  • One exosuit per player, not armies or unit management
  • 60-120 minutes, not 3+ hour sessions
  • Card-based rules, no codex bloat
  • Play official or community-built scenarios, or design your own using the modular mission framework
